OUR CURRENT FLAVOURS

WHAT MAKES US SPECIAL

Here at Nomu Creamery we make the world's most delicious ice cream made completely free of animal products and its derivatives. We believe we can still enjoy ice cream while not exploiting animals and putting strain on the environment and our health. We use only the finest ingredients for our ice cream, even making the nut milks that make up our creamy bases in house. We source premium ingredients for the flavours and delicious mix-ins and always make fresh batches each week to ensure the best tasting treat on the planet. We are positive you will love our ice cream and hope you will bring your friends and family to try us too!

OUR SHOP

Nomu is located on trendy 124st in downtown Edmonton. Our space is 900 square feet with plenty of natural light and seating for guests to enjoy their ice cream. Our location has plenty of parking in order lot around back and on the street. During the summer, we break out the patio and are open late on the warm summer nights.

During the build of our shop, it was crucial to us to use materials that were ethically and sustainably sourced, as well as environmentally friendly. Nomu Creamery has an intense dedication to sourcing ingredients from sustainable sources and always to find them as close to home as possible. We are a net zero shop and all of the energy needed to run the shop is produced by solar panels on the roof.
